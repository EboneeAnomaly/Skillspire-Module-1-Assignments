About In the late 1940's, Twin Peaks's beloved Department Store magnate, J.J. Horne, opened The Great Northern Hotel. Desiring a destination where guests could take in the natural majesty Twin Peaks's offers, he situated his vision on the bluffs above White Tail Falls. His son, Benjamin Horne, succeded his father in running the establishment in 1980, where he continues to run it to this day.

Town Nestled between the White Tail Mountain and Blue Pine Mountain, Twin Peaks (which draws its name from this geographical fact) is a small logging community in northeast Washington State. Though small, it boasts many fabulous amenities such as lush hiking trails, local artisan offerings, community minded atmosphere, beautiful vistas, and a rich, cultural history. We cordially invite you to put your feet up in mountainscape luxury - The Hornes
